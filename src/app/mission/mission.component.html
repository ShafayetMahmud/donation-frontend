“Al-Mahad As-Sabur” means “The Institute for Allah [the All-Patient]”

Our journey to worship Allah is eternal, in this world and in Jannah. Mankind, while on earth, needs to live their lives and establish themselves as the rightful successors of Adam to Muhammand (Sallallahu Alaihi wa Sallam). The individuals involved in organizing and those benefiting from the institute will work with the sole mission of pleasing Allah Subhanahu wa Ta’ala, following Al-Qur'an and the Sunnah of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (Sallallahu Alaihi wa Sallam).

The institute will sustain itself through its own income sources and Zakah from its benefactors, using these resources to provide shelter and education for the needy and orphaned children. Since Zakah will be utilized, expenditures will be strictly within the eight categories where Zakah is permissible.
Children, typically between the ages of 6-8 years and 16-18 years, will receive education centered around the Quran, including: Al-Qur’an (both memorization as Hafiz and understanding its meanings and Tafsir), As-Sunnah and Fiqh, Alongside worldly education in subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and more. They will also learn technical training and business management.
Through this comprehensive education, students will grow into individuals who understand how to please Allah Subhanahu wa Ta’ala, contribute to future generations, and become leaders in society.
In Sha Allah, the students will be equipped to pursue higher education at any esteemed institution of their choice, whether it be BUET, RUET, DU, Medical Colleges, Al-Azhar University, or Al-Medina University, among others. In Sha Allah, they will be ready to start a new business or engage in professional life, if they choose to do so.
“I did not create jinn and humans except to worship Me.” [51:56]
