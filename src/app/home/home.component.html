Why Al-Mahad As-Saboor?

Our main goals:
• provide shelter and education to orphans
• spread Islamic upbringing to grow a better society
• get rid of the divide between Islamic track and mainstream track

‘Al Mahad As Sabur’ means ‘The Institute for Allah’. Sabur refers to The All-Patient, one of the beautiful names of Allah Subhanahu wa Ta’ala.

Children who are orphaned or needy, typically 6–18 years old, will receive a Quran-centered education. Through this education, students grow into individuals who understand how to please Allah and become leaders in society.
