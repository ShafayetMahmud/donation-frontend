Why Al-Mahad As-Saboor? Our main goals:
provide shelter and education to orphans
spread Islamic upbringing to grow a better society
get rid of the divide between Islamic track and mainstream track ‘Al Mahad As Sabur ’ means ‘The Institute for Allah ’. Sabur refers to The All-Patient , one of the beautiful names of Allah Subhanahu wa Ta’ala.

Children who are orphaned or needy (and thus fall under the category of Zakat receipients), typically between the ages of 6-8 years and 16-18 years, will receive an education centered around the Quran. Through this comprehensive education, students will grow into individuals who understand how to please Allah Subhanahu wa Ta’ala and become leaders in society.
